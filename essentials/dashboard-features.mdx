Welcome to the Dialgen Dashboard! This section provides an overview of the various features available to manage your AI agents, campaigns, and monitor call analytics.

The main dashboard provides a quick glance at your operational metrics:

*   **Total Agents:** Number of active AI agents.
*   **Active Calls:** Real-time count of ongoing calls.
*   **Lead Conversions:** Track successful conversions from your campaigns.
*   **Daily Call Duration:** Aggregate duration of calls per day.
*   **Detailed Analytics:** Access in-depth transcription and sentiment analysis for each call.

Dialgen offers a wide range of customizable voice options for your AI agents to ensure a natural and engaging conversation experience.

*   **Pre-built Voices:** Choose from a library of high-quality, human-like voices.
*   **Custom Voice Cloning:** (Enterprise Feature) Upload voice samples to create a unique, branded voice for your agents.
*   **Voice Parameters:** Adjust parameters such as:

    *   **Speed:** Modify the speaking rate of the agent.
    *   **Emotional Tone:** Fine-tune the sentiment (e.g., friendly, formal, empathetic).

## ‚öôÔ∏è Advanced Settings

The advanced settings section allows you to fine-tune your agents and campaigns for optimal performance.

### Agent Configuration

*   **System Prompts:** Define the core persona and guidelines for your AI agent.
*   **Agent Instructions:** Provide specific instructions for handling different scenarios during a call.
*   **Custom Metrics:** Configure custom metrics to track specific outcomes relevant to your business (e.g., `intent`, `sentiment`, `customerSatisfaction`).

### Campaign Settings

*   **Rate Limiting:** Control the `maxCallsPerSecond` to manage call volume.
*   **Retry Strategy:** Configure parameters for `maxAttempts`, `noAnswerDelay`, and `busyDelay` for failed calls.
*   **Webhooks:** Set up `onBatchComplete`, `onCallComplete`, and `onBatchFailed` webhooks for real-time notifications.
*   **Scheduling:** Schedule campaigns to start at a future `scheduledStartTime` with specified `timezone`.

### Security & Integrations

*   **API Key Management:** Generate and manage your API keys.
*   **Webhook Security:** Implement authentication tokens for secure webhook endpoints.
*   **CRM Integrations:** Connect Dialgen with your existing CRM systems for seamless data flow.
